# Page Descriptions

## Home Page
The Home page introduces users to Bharat Suraksha with a hero banner, benefits overview, featured plans, and quick access to primary actions.


## Plans Page
The Plans page lists available health insurance plans. Users can explore coverage options, compare features, and choose the plan that suits their needs.


## Claims Page
The Claims page guides users through the complete claims process. It includes steps to file a claim, required documents, timelines, and a support section.



## About Us Page
The About Us page highlights the mission, values, experience, and trust elements of Bharat Suraksha, helping build user confidence in the brand.


## Contact Page
The Contact page provides users with assistance options such as a contact form, customer support email, phone number, and office address.


## Login Page
The Login page allows returning users to securely access their policy information, update personal details, and track claims status.


## Get Started Page
The Get Started page initiates the onboarding process, where users can begin selecting or customizing insurance plans based on their requirements.
